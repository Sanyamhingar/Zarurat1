Zarurat, as the name and emblem signifies “helping the deprived”, is a non-profit student-run organization whose main motive is to help the underprivileged children residing in nearby neighborhood, by providing them with the basic need and right of a human, i.e. primary education. The philosophy of the team is “each one; teach one”. Zarurat helps in overall development of a child and provides a platform where a child can educate himself / herself and hone his skills and become a better citizen as well as a better version of themselves. The foundation of Zarurat was laid in the year 2011 in JECRC Foundation and in the subsequent year it was brought to JECRC University by its first batch of students. In the initial days, surveys were conducted in the nearby areas of the campus to get a rough estimate of the number of children and accordingly the classes were started. Regular classes are being conducted 5 days a week which include a G.K class on each on every wednesday of the week. Also every Saturday we plan extra-curricular activities like sports, dance, music, art & craft are planned for the kids. Zarurat is a place where we not only educate the kids but also enrich them with lots of Cultural and Religious values. We as a Family celebrate almost all the festivals with the kids with the spirit of enthusiasm and commemoration. It instills in them the value of culture, integrity, secularity and harmony. Apart from this, events like Tree Plantation Drive, Diya Decoration, Medical Health check-up Drive, Clothes Distribution Event, Mask distribution, Annual Event, etc., are organized every year for the children. Also every year we make sure that we give at least 3-4 outings to the kids to the places like Jaipur Zoo, Monuments etc which provides a sense of enjoyment and also acts as a source of knowledge for them because no matter what our primary goal is to give them a good education. Zarurat volunteers also pay regular visits to old age homes, schools for deaf and dumb and a government school in the nearby village.Not only this, our Coordinators and volunteers go to the nearby Government schools each day to teach the kids over there because we focus on providing a quality education to as much students as we can. Zarurat has come up with a Basic Computer Knowledge program for underprivileged children. During the pandemic also, the team was connected with these children in online mode and delivered different E-Modules for their growth.

Our Events

Navyug

Navyug is an event which is organized by Team Abhyudaya Zarurat where over 300+ children along with their teachers from various government schools, including Government School Goner, Government School Vidhani, Khoj Public School, and JECRC Foundation. The event also garnered enthusiastic participation from the neighboring villages, making it a truly community-centered celebration. The fest featured is a diverse range of activities aimed at engaging and entertaining the participants. More than 12 indoor and outdoor games were conducted, complemented by over five cultural performances, showcasing the children's talents and teamwork. Additionally, 300+ goodies were distributed, heightening the day's excitement. This event embodied the spirit of collaboration and community, offering a platform for students to interact, compete, and revel in a supportive and festive atmosphere.

Diya Decoration and Exhibition

Every year at the time of Diwali, our incredible team hosts a vibrant fundraising event that truly highlights our creativity and dedication. During this heartwarming event, our volunteers pour their hearts into hand-painting over 6,000 diyas, and 200+ Art and Craft Items showcasing their amazing artistic talents. We are thrilled to share that we sell over 5,700 of these beautiful diyas, greatly contributing to our fundraising goals. Additionally, we show our gratitude to the helpers who support the event by gifting more than 200 diyas. The event wraps up with a truly heartwarming goodies distribution ceremony for the kids. This wonderful annual initiative not only raises funds but also fosters a beautiful sense of community, creativity, and generosity among the participants, making it a tradition that we hold close to our hearts.

Vastra Samman Sammaroh

Every year in December, a dedicated clothing distribution campaign is held to provide warmth and support to various communities. Over the course of four days, more than 900 sets of clothes are distributed to those in need. Boxes are placed in the buildings of JU, where students and faculties donate their clothes. On Day 1, over 400 sets are given to residents in Goner and Vidhani villages, as well as kids at JU. Day 2 focuses on providing 200+ sets at key hospitals, including RUHS, Mahatma Gandhi Hospital, Jeevan Rekha Hospital, and SMS Hospital. Day 3 sees the distribution of 200+ sets to the helpers at JECRC University. On Day 4, the campaign reaches out to children at JECRC College and nearby areas, providing over 100 sets. This annual initiative reflects our ongoing commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of those in our community.

Our Team

Why join us?

    Joining our student-run initiative offers a unique and rewarding opportunity to make a direct impact on the lives of underprivileged children. Here’s why you should consider becoming part of our team:

  • Meaningful Impact:

    Our mission is to support and uplift underprivileged kids, providing them with the resources and opportunities they need to thrive. By joining us, you become an integral part of this mission, helping to create lasting positive change in their lives.

  • Youth-Driven Innovation:

    As a student-run organization, we bring fresh perspectives and innovative ideas to the table. You’ll have the chance to contribute your creativity and enthusiasm to projects that are designed and executed by young people, for young people.

  • Hands-On Experience:

    You’ll gain valuable hands-on experience in various aspects of non-profit work, from organizing events and fundraising to direct service and outreach. This practical experience will be invaluable for your personal and professional growth.

  • Leadership and Skill Development:

    Our initiative provides a platform for you to develop leadership skills, teamwork, and project management abilities. You’ll be working alongside like-minded peers, allowing you to grow in a supportive and collaborative environment.

  • Community and Support:

    As part of our initiative, you’ll join a passionate and supportive community of fellow students and mentors who are dedicated to the cause. You’ll be part of a network that values your contributions and supports your growth.

  • Inspiration and Motivation:

    Working with underprivileged kids can be incredibly inspiring. Their resilience and potential will motivate you to work harder and strive for greater impact, enriching your own life and perspective in the process.
  • By joining us, you’re not just contributing to a cause—you’re becoming part of a movement driven by young people who are committed to making a difference. Together, we can create opportunities, inspire change, and empower underprivileged kids to achieve their full potential.

Recruitment Process

Application form release

We release Application forms at the time of Starting of the new session, which remain open for 1 week.

Formation of Whatsapp group

After Receiving applications, we make whatsapp group to share updates regading further process and updates.

Introduction Ceremony

Event to make you familier with the Working of Zarurat and Introduce you to the team.

Interview round-1

First round of Interviews are taken by 3rd and 4th year of Zaurat to know about you, your basic problem solving skills and how you can contribute. (No elimination round).

Observation Period

For over 1 month we observe you. Such as, your dedication, team work, behaviour towards each other, etc,.

Interview Round-2

After observations we check your attendance and what you observed in the entire time period. We have verticals like Social media, Content Technical, Graphic, Cultural and Art and Craft team.

voilà, You're now part of Zarurat

After passing all the process you become the permanent team member of Zarurat.
